This is SRM INSIDER - Information Guide

What is SRM INSIDER?
SRM Insider is a premier community platform dedicated to fostering innovation, entrepreneurship, and professional development among students and young professionals. It serves as a bridge between academic learning and real-world industry experience. The platform focuses on creating meaningful connections and opportunities for personal growth.

When did it start?
SRM Insider was founded in 2020 as an initiative to create a dynamic ecosystem for learning and growth. Since its inception, it has grown to become one of the most active and vibrant communities on campus, with hundreds of members participating in various events and programs.

Who is the founder of SRM INSIDER?
SRM Insider was founded by a group of visionary students and faculty members from SRM Institute of Science and Technology who believed in the power of community-driven learning and peer mentorship. Their vision was to create a platform where students could collaborate, innovate, and grow together.

What are the domains of SRM INSIDER?
SRM Insider operates across multiple domains including:
Technology and Innovation: Artificial Intelligence, Web Development, Mobile Applications, Cloud Computing, and emerging technologies.
Entrepreneurship: Startup Development, Business Planning, Pitch Competitions, and investor networking.
Leadership: Management Skills, Team Building, Public Speaking, and communication excellence.
Professional Development: Internships, Career Guidance, Interview Preparation, and skill enhancement.
Social Impact: Community Service, Sustainability Projects, Social Entrepreneurship, and community outreach.

What is SRM Roomie?
SRM Roomie is an innovative service platform launched by SRM Insider to help students find compatible roommates and manage accommodation efficiently. It uses smart matching algorithms to connect students based on their preferences, lifestyle habits, academic schedules, and budget constraints. The platform also provides tools for roommate agreements, expense sharing, and conflict resolution to ensure a harmonious living experience. SRM Roomie aims to make student housing more convenient, affordable, and enjoyable while fostering friendships among roommates.
